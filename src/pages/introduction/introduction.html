Welcome to
Gluco

Gluco applies artificial intelligence and machine learning to your Diabetes care.

Pattern
Recognition

Gluco identifies both frequent day patterns and meal based patterns in your blood glucose results in order to produce a recommendation to you on how to fix them as well as suggesting potential causes.

Insulin Dose
Recommendations

Gluco recommends your optimal insulin dose based upon your current blood glucose, physical activity level and carbohydrate consumption.

Gluco learns from the outcomes of your previous blood glucose results in order to constantly evolve over time and tailor itself to your individual glucose metabolism.
